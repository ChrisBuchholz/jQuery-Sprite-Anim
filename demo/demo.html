Your Sprite Animation

This is a demonstration of your sprite animation. Have fun!

Example 1: Animation as data-attributes

HTML: 

<div class='spriteanim'
	data-baseurl='demo'
	data-grid='2x9'
	data-blocksize='1000x208'
	data-frames='47'
	data-fps='12'
	data-autoplay='true'
	data-autoload='true'
	data-retina='true'>
</div>

Example 2: Same as above with Retina-enabled and pause at frame 46 (47th frame) for 2 seconds

Javascript: 

<script>
	$('#spriteanim2').spriteanim().on('frame-19-shown', function() {
		$(this).spriteanim('stop');
		window.setTimeout(function() {
			$(this).spriteanim('play');
		}.bind(this), 2000)
	});
</script<
